About Dial a Question

Dial a Question started as a quest to fix the "Holiday Zoom Party". The goal was to create an inclusive game that was fun for everyone, and low-anxiety for the introverts among us.

What started as one game naturally evolved into a full suite of thought-provoking conversation starters:

  • Dial a Question: The original flagship game, now tailored for a variety of audiences.
  • Would You Rather: A fresh take on the classic, with 13,000+ combinations of mildly annoying choices.
  • Scenes From a Hat: The rapid-fire improv game inspired by Whose Line is it Anyway?
  • Beat the Ding: A fast-paced tribute to the long-running New Zealand TV show 7 Days.

Real Prompts for Real Connection.

Every prompt in the Dial a Question suite is hand-curated to spark genuine creativity and connection. This site is built for people, not profit. There are no ads, no memberships, and no data collection. In a world of digital isolation and constant monetization, this is a space built specifically to bring you closer to your coworkers, friends, family, or even the stranger next to you in line at the DMV.

I hope these games help you find a little more laughter and connection with the people around you.
